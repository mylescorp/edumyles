## Recommended Pilot Selection Approach

Do not activate all schools at once.

Start with:

- 2 from Kiambu
- 2 from Kitengela
- 1 to 2 from Nairobi

Selection criteria:

- fast response time
- decision-maker access
- readiness to share data
- willingness to test with real staff and parents
- moderate student count for the first wave
